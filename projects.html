Projects

We work with different people. There is a project that we are working on. It is called "date". Date is when the couple comes to our studio and create together. We offer them food, tea, sweets, and etc. If there is a teacher present he tries to be invisible because people came on the date to spend time together, and bond their attachment to each other.

Families

You can also come to us with your family. We want you to spend time together. We understand the importance of spending family time together because many people forget about it. We want to make a culture from it, so people will come here on holidays.
